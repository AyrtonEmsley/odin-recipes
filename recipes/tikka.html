Chicken Tikka

Description

This Chicken Tikka recipe is rich in flavors and easy to make. It's a popular dish in Indian cuisine that's perfect for any occasion. The chicken is marinated with yogurt and spices and grilled to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp tikka masala powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix the yogurt, tikka masala powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, garlic, ginger, salt, lemon juice, and vegetable oil.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and mix well. Cover and let it marinate in the fridge for at least 2 hours, or overnight for better flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers and grill for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is fully cooked and slightly charred on the edges.
  5. Remove the chicken from the skewers and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  6. Serve with naan or rice and enjoy!
