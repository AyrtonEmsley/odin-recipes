Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is easy to follow and perfect for any occasion. It’s packed with flavor and full of cheese!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef and onion in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  4. In a large baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
